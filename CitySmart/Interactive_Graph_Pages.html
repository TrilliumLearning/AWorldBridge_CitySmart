Kodiak High School

The Kodiak High School wind turbine produces about 120 kilowatts per month. This Skystream wind turbine costs $3,000 and includes monitoring software so that data can be monitored and recorded remotely. Kodiak's - A World Bridge students have connected to the wind turbine's data stream via radio transmitter, then relay this data through a CPU in the World Bridge lab that parses the output to send finalized data to a host server.
