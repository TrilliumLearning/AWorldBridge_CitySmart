KHS Wind Turbine Information

The Kodiak High School wind turbine is located next to the Borough building across from the high school and produces about 120 kilowatts per month. This specific wind turbine costs 3,000 and includes monitoring software so that data can be monitored and recorded from within the home, business, or school it is located at. Kodiak High school students have connected to it via radio link and relay this data through a raspberry pi that parses the output to send finalized data to a nearby server.
